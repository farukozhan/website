WHO WE ARE  

Imperial International Trade, LLC was founded in Brooklyn, New York in 2017, with a goal to bring Turkish manufacturers to international markets. We distribute products as varied as home decoration, textiles and automotive parts, with the list ever expanding. Our company specializes in providing a tailored service at competitive price.

The company has enjoyed steady, organic growth based upon our quality service and customer satisfaction. Alongside the auto spare parts range, we have recently launched a home range under the brand" Uncle Geppetto  ".             

MISSION

We are committed to helping our suppliers reach an international market, and providing best value for our customers. We will provide " Innovative, Modern, Punctual, Exclusive, Responsible, International, Accountable, Long lasting " service and products to clients.

 

 

 

VISION

Our vison is to bring boutique Turkish brands to the international market, and be the first choice for our customers.
